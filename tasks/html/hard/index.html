About Us

Welcome to Fork, Commit, Merge, your one-stop destination to embark on an exciting journey into the world of open-source contributions on GitHub! We understand that getting started with open source can be a daunting task, and that's where we come in. Our project is here to guide you, step by step, through the entire contribution workflow, making it easier for you to become a part of the vibrant open-source community.

Services

At Fork, Commit, Merge, we offer a comprehensive suite of services to empower you on your open-source journey. Our platform provides guided workflows that take you through every step of the contribution process, ensuring that you understand and master the open-source landscape. You won't be working on simulated projects; instead, you'll dive into real, impactful initiatives, gaining hands-on experience and contributing to real-world solutions.
